# PRD — DRONEWATCH: Soldier Drone Alert System

## Product Vision
A 60-second MP4 marketing/explainer video demonstrating DRONEWATCH — a ground-based sound and motion detection system that protects IDF soldiers from fiber optic drone attacks by issuing an automated siren alert within a 400–500 meter detection radius.

## Problem Statement
Fiber optic drones are a lethal, precise, low-signature threat on the modern battlefield. Soldiers have minimal reaction time and no reliable early warning system. The drone attacks before soldiers can seek cover.

## Solution
DRONEWATCH detects incoming drones via acoustic and motion sensors. Upon detection within a 400–500m radius, the system immediately triggers a siren alert — giving soldiers 15–30 seconds to take cover inside armored vehicles or fortified structures. The drone reaches the zone and finds an empty battlefield.

## Video Structure (60 seconds)

| Scene | Duration | Title | Content |
|-------|----------|-------|---------|
| 1 — The Threat | 0–20s | THREAT DETECTED | Soldiers on open battlefield, fiber optic drone appears, soldiers scramble and exposed |
| 2 — The Solution | 20–40s | DRONEWATCH ACTIVATED | Radar pulse expands 400–500m, siren triggers, soldiers move to cover |
| 3 — The Result | 40–60s | SOLDIERS PROTECTED | Empty battlefield, drone hits a wall, soldiers safe inside armored vehicles |
